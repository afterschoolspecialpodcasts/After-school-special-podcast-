Meet the Crew

A Aaron DD Double D S Staffman

Three Detroit guys who couldn't stop having the same conversation — so they hit record. Aaron brings the sports takes and the moral compass. Double D brings the receipts on everything that aired before 2005. Staffman brings the energy, the wildcard opinions, and absolutely zero chill.

Together they've built over 106 episodes of the most Detroit podcast you'll ever hear — raw, funny, and completely unscripted. Throwback culture isn't nostalgia for them. It's a lifestyle.
