✦ About the show

The show that takes you back to the good stuff

After School Special Podcast is a nostalgia comedy podcast hosted by three lifelong friends — Double D, Staffman, and Aaron — who get together every Friday to relive the pop culture, TV shows, music, sneakers, and childhood memories that shaped a generation.

From deep dives into 90s sitcoms like Fresh Prince of Bel-Air to the history of Air Jordans and sneaker culture, from Saturday morning cartoons to iconic music videos of the early 2000s — no topic is off limits and nothing is too nostalgic. Whether you grew up watching BET, MTV, or Nick at Nite, this is the podcast for you.

With over 109 episodes, a 5-star rating on Apple Podcasts, and a growing community of Latchkey Kids and Broken Homies, After School Special Podcast is one of the most entertaining nostalgia podcasts on the internet. New episodes drop every Friday — subscribe on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, or YouTube so you never miss one.

What We Talk About

📺

90s & 2000s TV Shows

From Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and Martin to The Wire and Chappelle's Show — we break down the episodes, the moments, and the culture behind the shows that defined our generation.

👟

Sneaker Culture & Fashion

Air Jordans, Timberlands, LA Lights, and the sneakers kids got robbed for — we dig into the history of street fashion and what our shoes said about who we were growing up.

🎵

Music & Music Videos

MTV, BET, DMX, Missy Elliott, Thong Song, and everything in between. We revisit the music videos and artists that had us glued to the TV and rewinding cassette tapes.

🏀

Sports & Pop Culture Icons

Michael Jordan, Allen Iverson, the Bad Boys Pistons — we cover the athletes and icons whose influence went way beyond sports and into fashion, music, and everyday life.

😂

Comedy & Real Talk

It's not all nostalgia — the guys bring real opinions, hilarious debates, and unfiltered conversations about life, relationships, current events, and whatever is on their minds that week.

🎙️

Guest Interviews

Special guests including producers, military veterans, psychologists, and community figures join the crew to share stories that go beyond nostalgia and deep into real life experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is After School Special Podcast about?

After School Special Podcast is a nostalgia and comedy podcast about the pop culture, TV shows, music, sneakers, and memories of the 80s, 90s and early 2000s. Hosted by Double D, Staffman, and Aaron, the show covers everything from Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and Air Jordans to music videos and childhood experiences, with new episodes every Friday.

Where can I listen to After School Special Podcast?

After School Special Podcast is available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, and anywhere you get your podcasts. New episodes are released every Friday. You can also watch full video episodes on the YouTube channel @afterschoolspecialpodcast.

How many episodes does After School Special Podcast have?

After School Special Podcast has over 109 episodes covering a wide range of nostalgic and pop culture topics. The show has been running since 2021 and releases new episodes every Friday.

Who are the hosts of After School Special Podcast?

After School Special Podcast is hosted by three friends known as Double D, Staffman, and Aaron. They grew up together and bring their shared memories and unfiltered opinions to every conversation about nostalgia, pop culture, and everything in between.

Is After School Special Podcast highly rated?
