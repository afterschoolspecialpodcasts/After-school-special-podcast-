Who We Are

About the Show

After School Special Podcast started as a conversation between three Detroit guys who couldn't stop talking about the movies, music, sports, and moments that shaped their childhoods. What started in a living room turned into a full-blown show dedicated to anyone who's ever said "they don't make 'em like that anymore."

Every episode, Aaron, Double D, and Staffman dig into the culture that defined the 80s, 90s, and early 2000s — from Saturday morning cartoons and classic video games to Detroit-specific legends and the music that soundtracked our youth.

Rooted in Detroit and broadcasting to the world, After School Special is for everyone who grew up too fast and wants to slow it back down for an hour or two.
